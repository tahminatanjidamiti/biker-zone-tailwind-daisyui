Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How do you prevent motorcycle tires from wearing out quickly?

Regularly check tire pressure, avoid aggressive braking, and rotate tires if possible to prevent uneven wear and extend tire life.

How often should motorcycle oil be changed?

Oil should be changed every 3,000 to 5,000 miles, depending on riding conditions and manufacture recommendations.

Q: How do you properly maintain a motorcycle chain?

Clean and lubricate the chain every 500 miles or after riding in wet or dirty conditions to ensure longevity.
